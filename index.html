Assistant Professor of Software Engineering,

Turin Polytechnic University in Tashkent

Jaloliddin is an assistant professor at the Turin Polytechnic University in Tashkent. His primary research interests include formal verification of security policies in automated network orchestration. His other research interests include modeling, cyber physical systems, and cloud computing systems.
